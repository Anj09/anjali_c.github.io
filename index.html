Who I Am

Curious mind.
Creative problem-solver.

I'm Anjali — a researcher and data scientist fascinated by the intersections of biology-inspired computing, precision agriculture, and language models. I completed my M.Tech in CS with specialisation in AI & ML at VIT, and am currently pursuing an MRes in Advanced AI at the University of Sussex, Brighton.

At the University of Sussex's Computational Neuroscience Lab, I work with Spiking Neural Networks, exploring learning dynamics and STDP variants. My dissertation investigates semantic and phonetic overlap and bias mitigation in LLMs.

Beyond research, I love poetry, sketching, badminton, and the occasional competitive throwball match.
